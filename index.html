Meet the Designer

Meet Shirley Moon

With over 40 years of practice spanning residential estates, boutique hospitality, and Class-A commercial interiors, Shirley Moon has become one of Dallas–Fort Worth’s most respected design voices. Her mastery of color psychology and material harmony has earned the trust of Fortune 500 CEOs, leading builders, and discerning homeowners alike.

A pioneer in evidence-based color consulting, Shirley’s process combines deep listening with scientific precision — ensuring every space she touches not only looks extraordinary, but feels exactly right.
